<p><p>The CO2 stunning system market includes two primary types: continuous gas systems and disposable gas systems. Continuous gas systems involve a constant supply of CO2 for stunning animals, making them ideal for large-scale operations. On the other hand, disposable gas systems use pre-filled cylinders that are discarded after use, making them convenient for smaller operations or temporary setups. Both types of systems offer effective ways to stun animals before processing in slaughterhouses.</p></p>
