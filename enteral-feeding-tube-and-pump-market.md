<p><p>Enteral feeding tube and pump market types include Nasoenteric Feeding Tube, Nasogastric Tubes, Gastrostomy Tube, Suction Catheters, and Tube Feed Pumps. Nasoenteric feeding tubes are inserted through the nose into the small intestine for long-term use. Nasogastric tubes are inserted through the nose into the stomach for short-term feeding. Gastrostomy tubes are inserted directly into the stomach through a surgical procedure. Suction catheters are used for suctioning fluids from the body. Tube feed pumps are used to deliver nutrition through the enteral feeding tube.</p></p>
