<p><p>Eye tracking solutions come in various market types including Eye Attached Tracking, Optical Tracking, and Electrooculography. Eye Attached Tracking involves tracking eye movement using an external camera attached to the eye. Optical Tracking uses infrared light and cameras to track eye movement. Electrooculography measures the electrical potential between different parts of the eye to track movement. Each type of eye tracking solution offers unique benefits and applications for research, marketing, and human-computer interaction.</p></p>
