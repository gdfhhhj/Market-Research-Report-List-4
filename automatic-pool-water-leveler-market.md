<p><p>Automatic pool water levelers are essential accessories for maintaining an optimal water level in swimming pools. There are two main market types for these products - above ground use and in-ground use. Above ground use water levelers are specifically designed for pools that are above the ground level, such as inflatable or metal frame pools. In-ground use water levelers, on the other hand, are designed for pools that are built into the ground, such as fiberglass or concrete pools. Both types serve the same purpose of keeping the water level constant.</p></p>
