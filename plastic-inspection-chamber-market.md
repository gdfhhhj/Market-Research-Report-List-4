<p><strong>The Plastic Inspection Chamber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotational Molding Plastic Inspection Chamber</li><li>Welded Piping Plastic Inspection Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic Inspection Chamber market is divided into two main types - Rotational Molding and Welded Piping. Rotational Molding plastic chambers are produced using a heating mechanism where the plastic is melted and then rotated inside a mold to create the desired shape. Welded Piping plastic chambers, on the other hand, are constructed by fusing together different sections of plastic piping. Both types offer advantages such as durability, corrosion resistance, and ease of installation, making them popular choices in the construction industry.</p></p>
