<p><p>Dental acrylic market is segmented into four types- Heat Cure Acrylic Resin, Self-Curing Acrylic Resin, Cold Cure Acrylic Resin, and Light-Cured Acrylic Resin. Heat Cure Acrylic Resins require heat for polymerization, providing strong and durable prosthetics. Self-Curing Acrylic Resins polymerize by themselves at room temperature, helpful for quick procedures. Cold Cure Acrylic Resins are for prosthetics that may be damaged by heat, curing at lower temperatures. Light-Cured Acrylic Resins use UV light for polymerization, offering a quick and efficient method.</p></p>
