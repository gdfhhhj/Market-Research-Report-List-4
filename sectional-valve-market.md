<p><p>Sectional valves can be categorized into two main types: sectional directional valves and proportional directional valves. Sectional directional valves are used to control the direction of fluid flow in hydraulic systems, allowing for precise control over the movement of hydraulic actuators. Proportional directional valves, on the other hand, allow for variable and precise control of flow rate and direction, making them ideal for applications where a high degree of control is required. Both types of sectional valves play a crucial role in various hydraulic systems.</p></p>
