<p><p>Electrochemical titrators market offers different types of titrators such as Potentiometric Titrators, Volumetric Titrators, Coulometric Titrators, and others. Potentiometric titrators measure the potential difference between a reference electrode and a measuring electrode. Volumetric titrators measure the volume of titrant required to reach the endpoint. Coulometric titrators measure the quantity of electricity required to complete the titration. Other types of titrators may include Karl Fischer titrators for moisture determination or amperometric titrators for specific ion analysis.</p></p>
