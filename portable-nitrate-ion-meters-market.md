<p><p>Portable Nitrate Ion Meters come in two main types: Single Input and Dual Input. Single Input meters are designed to measure nitrate ion concentration from one sample at a time, providing quick and accurate results for individual samples. Dual Input meters, on the other hand, are capable of measuring nitrate ion concentration from two separate samples simultaneously, allowing for comparison between samples or different measurement conditions. Both types of meters offer portable and convenient solutions for on-site nitrate ion measurement applications.</p></p>
