<p><p>Video intercom devices and equipment market can be categorized into two main types: Analog Type and IP Type. Analog type video intercom systems rely on traditional analog signals to transmit audio and video feed from the door entry panel to the indoor unit. On the other hand, IP type video intercom systems use digital technology to transmit data over an IP network, allowing for higher video quality and more advanced features such as remote access and integration with other smart home devices. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications.</p></p>
