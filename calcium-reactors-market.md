<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Structure</li><li>Dual Chamber Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Calcium reactors are devices used in the marine aquarium industry to maintain proper levels of calcium and alkalinity in the water. There are two main types of calcium reactors on the market: single chamber structure and dual chamber structure. Single chamber reactors have one chamber where the calcium carbonate media is dissolved, while dual chamber reactors have separate chambers for the media and the water. Dual chamber reactors are typically more efficient and easier to use, but single chamber reactors are simpler and more cost-effective for smaller aquariums.</p></p>
