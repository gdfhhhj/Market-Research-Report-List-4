<p><p>Clamp multimeters are available in two main market types: digital and simulation. Digital clamp multimeters display measurements digitally on a screen, making them easy to read and accurate. Simulation clamp multimeters, on the other hand, replicate the functionality of real-world circuits to test and analyze electrical systems. Both types play a crucial role in the electrical industry, with digital clamp multimeters being popular for everyday use and simulation clamp multimeters being utilized for more complex testing and analysis tasks.</p></p>
