<p><ul><li>Insulation Type</li><li>Sintered Type</li><li>Heat Curing Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip scale adhesive market can be categorized into three main types: insulation type, sintered type, and heat curing type. Insulation type adhesives provide electrical insulation between the chip and substrate. Sintered type adhesives use heat and pressure to bond the chip to the substrate. Heat curing adhesives require additional heat treatment to cure and create a strong bond between the chip and substrate. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
