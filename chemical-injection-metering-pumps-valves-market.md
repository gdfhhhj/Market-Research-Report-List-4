<p><p>Chemical injection metering pumps & valves are used in various industries to accurately control the flow of chemicals into a process. The market is categorized into three main types: diaphragm, piston/plunger, and others. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible membrane to push the chemical through the system. Piston/plunger pumps utilize a reciprocating piston to achieve the desired flow rate. Other types of pumps may include gear pumps or peristaltic pumps. Each type has its own unique features and applications in the chemical injection industry.</p></p>
