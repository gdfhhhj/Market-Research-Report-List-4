<p><p>Centrifugal dryers are categorized into different types based on their mechanism and application. High-Speed Centrifugal Spray Dryers utilize a high-speed rotating disk to atomize liquid feed into fine droplets for rapid drying. Industrial Centrifugal Dryers are specifically designed for large-scale industrial applications, offering high efficiency and throughput. Each type of centrifugal dryer caters to specific drying needs and is widely used in various industries for quick and efficient drying processes.</p></p>
