<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electronic Multiplier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM)</li><li>Continuous-dynode multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM) are a type of electronic multiplier that consists of a series of channels or segments where electrons are multiplied through secondary emission. These multipliers are used in various applications such as mass spectrometry, ion mobility spectrometry, and particle detectors.</p><p>Continuous-dynode multipliers, on the other hand, are electronic multipliers with a continuous dynode structure that allows for even greater amplification of electrons. These multipliers are used in applications requiring higher sensitivity and detection efficiency.</p></p>
