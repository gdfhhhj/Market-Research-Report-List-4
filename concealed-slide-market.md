<p><p>Concealed slide markets can be classified into two main types: full extension and single extension. Full extension slides allow the drawer to fully extend out of the cabinet, providing easy access to the entire contents of the drawer. Single extension slides, on the other hand, only extend partially out of the cabinet, limiting access to the back of the drawer. Both types serve specific purposes, with full extension slides being more popular for applications requiring maximum accessibility and single extension slides being suitable for simpler applications.</p></p>
