<p><p>An oil free diaphragm pump is a type of pump that does not require lubrication with oil during operation, making it ideal for applications where oil contamination is not desired. These pumps utilize a diaphragm to create suction and discharge pressures, with no contact between the internal moving parts and the pumped fluid. This design not only eliminates the risk of oil contamination but also reduces maintenance requirements and increases the pump's durability.</p><p>The oil free diaphragm pump market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period.
