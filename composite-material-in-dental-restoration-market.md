<p><p>Composite materials in dental restoration refer to materials made up of a combination of different components, such as resin-based composites and poly-acid modified composites. Resin-based composites are a popular choice in dental restorations due to their natural appearance and ability to bond securely to the tooth structure. Poly-acid modified composites, on the other hand, contain an acidic component that can help prevent recurrent decay and provide a more durable restoration. Both types of composite materials offer aesthetic and functional benefits for dental patients.</p></p>
