<p><p>Surgical lasers are classified into different types based on the medium used for generating the laser beam. They include CO2 Lasers, Argon Lasers, Nd:YAG Lasers, Diode Lasers, and other surgical lasers. CO2 lasers are commonly used for cutting and vaporizing tissues. Argon lasers are used in ophthalmology and dermatology procedures. Nd:YAG lasers are versatile and used for various procedures. Diode lasers are used for soft tissue surgeries. Other surgical lasers include thulium, holmium, and excimer lasers, each with specific applications in surgery.</p></p>
