<p><p>Resistive soil moisture sensors measure the resistance of the soil to determine its moisture content. These sensors are cost-effective and suitable for basic applications. Capacitive soil moisture sensors use a dielectric material to measure the soil's water content. They are more accurate and durable compared to resistive sensors, making them ideal for precision agriculture and research purposes. Both types of sensors are widely used in agricultural and environmental monitoring applications to optimize irrigation practices and improve crop yield.</p></p>
