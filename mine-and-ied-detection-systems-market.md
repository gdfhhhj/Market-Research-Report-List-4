<p><p>Mine and IED detection systems market can be classified into three types: sensor-based, radar-based, and laser-based systems. Sensor-based systems rely on detecting changes in physical parameters such as pressure, sound, or magnetic fields to identify potential threats. Radar-based systems use electromagnetic waves to detect objects underground or hidden from view. Laser-based systems utilize laser beams to scan and detect anomalies in the environment. Each type of detection system has its own strengths and limitations in accurately identifying mines and IEDs.</p></p>
