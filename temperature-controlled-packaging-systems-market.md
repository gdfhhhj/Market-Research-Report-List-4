<p><p>Temperature controlled packaging systems are used to maintain the desired temperature of products during transportation or storage. Active systems use external power sources to regulate temperature, while passive systems rely on phase change materials or insulating materials. Hybrid systems combine features of both active and passive systems to provide more precise temperature control. Each system has its own advantages and drawbacks, depending on factors such as the distance of transportation, duration, and required temperature range. Selecting the most suitable system is crucial to ensure product integrity and safety.</p></p>
