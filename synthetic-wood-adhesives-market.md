<p><p>Synthetic wood adhesives market is categorized into four types - solvent-based, water-based, solventless, and others. Solvent-based adhesives use organic solvents as a carrier, water-based adhesives use water as a carrier, while solventless adhesives do not contain any solvent. Other types may include hot melt adhesives or reactive hot melt adhesives. Each type of adhesive offers unique benefits and uses in the woodworking industry, depending on factors such as application method, drying time, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
