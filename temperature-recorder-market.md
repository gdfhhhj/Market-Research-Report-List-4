<p><p>Temperature recorders can be categorized into different types based on their functionality and connectivity options. Stand-alone data loggers are devices that record temperature data independently without requiring a connection to a computer or network. Web-based data loggers allow users to access temperature data remotely through a web interface. Wireless data loggers use wireless technology to transmit temperature data to a centralized system. BLE data loggers utilize Bluetooth Low Energy technology for data transmission, enabling easy connectivity with smartphones and other devices.</p></p>
