<p><ul><li>SideBull</li><li>FrontBull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ambulift market can be categorized into two main types: SideBull and FrontBull. SideBull ambulifts are designed to lift passengers from the side of the aircraft, providing easy access for those sitting in window seats. FrontBull ambulifts, on the other hand, are designed to lift passengers from the front of the aircraft, making them ideal for larger aircraft with multiple doors. Both types of ambulifts serve the same purpose of safely and efficiently boarding and deplaning passengers with reduced mobility.</p></p>
