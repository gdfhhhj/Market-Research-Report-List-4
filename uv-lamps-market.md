<p><p>UV lamps can be classified into two main types: High Boron Glass Lamps and Quartz Glass Lamps. High Boron Glass Lamps are known for their high UV transmission rates and are commonly used in applications where precise UV spectral output is required. On the other hand, Quartz Glass Lamps are preferred for their superior UV transmission properties and resistance to high temperatures. Both types of UV lamps play a crucial role in various industries, such as water treatment, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
