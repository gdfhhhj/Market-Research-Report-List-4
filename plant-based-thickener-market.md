<p><p>Plant based thickeners are substances derived from plants that are used to increase the viscosity of food products such as soups, sauces, and dressings. They are a popular alternative to traditional thickeners like cornstarch and flour, as they are often considered healthier and more environmentally friendly. Common plant based thickeners include arrowroot, tapioca, and guar gum.</p><p>The Plant Based Thickener Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.70% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for natural and plant-based ingredients in the food and beverage industry is a key driver for the growth of the plant based thickener market. Consumers are becoming more conscious about the ingredients in their food, leading to a growing preference for plant based thickeners over synthetic alternatives.</p><p>Another trend driving market growth is the rise of vegan and vegetarian diets, as plant based thickeners are often suitable for these dietary preferences. Additionally, the growing popularity of clean label products is boosting the demand for plant based thickeners, as they are perceived as clean label ingredients.</p><p>Overall, the plant based thickener market is expected to continue growing as consumers increasingly seek out natural, plant-based alternatives in their food products.</p></p>
