<p><p>Wind power transmission equipment market can be segmented into three main types: gearbox, coupling, and others. Gearboxes are essential components in wind turbines that help transfer the rotational energy from the blades to the generator. Couplings are used to connect different parts of the transmission system and ensure smooth power transfer. The "others" category includes various auxiliary equipment such as bearings, shafts, and brakes. These components work together to efficiently and effectively transmit power in wind turbine systems.</p></p>
