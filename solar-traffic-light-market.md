<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Mobile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed solar traffic lights are permanently installed at intersections or crossings, usually powered by solar panels mounted on a pole or nearby structure. They provide a reliable and consistent traffic control solution. On the other hand, mobile solar traffic lights are portable and can be easily moved to different locations as needed. They are powered by rechargeable batteries and are ideal for temporary traffic management situations such as road construction or special events. Both market types cater to the need for sustainable, energy-efficient traffic management solutions.</p></p>
