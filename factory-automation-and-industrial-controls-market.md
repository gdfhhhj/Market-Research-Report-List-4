<p><p>Factory Automation and Industrial Controls involve the use of various technologies and systems to control and operate machinery and equipment in manufacturing plants. Field Devices include sensors, actuators, and other devices that collect and transmit data, while Industrial Control Systems include programmable logic controllers (PLCs), distributed control systems (DCS), and supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA) systems that control the operation of the machinery based on the data collected from the field devices.</p></p>
