Histology/Cytology tests examine tissue samples under a microscope. Tumor Marker Immunoassays detect proteins in the blood that may indicate cancer. Point-of-care (POC) Colon Cancer Tests are convenient, rapid tests that can detect colon cancer at an early stage. Each type of test plays a crucial role in diagnosing and treating cancer effectively.</p></p>
