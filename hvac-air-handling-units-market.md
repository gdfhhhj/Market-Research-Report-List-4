<p><strong>The HVAC Air Handling Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Draw Through Type</li><li>Blow Through Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVAC Air Handling Units come in two main types: Draw Through Type and Blow Through Type. </p><p>In Draw Through Type units, the fan is positioned after the cooling coil, drawing air through the coil and then sending it into the ductwork. This type is less prone to condensation due to the coil being before the fan.</p><p>In Blow Through Type units, the fan is positioned before the cooling coil, blowing air through the coil and into the ductwork. This type is typically more compact and easier to install.</p></p>
