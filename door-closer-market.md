<p><strong>The Door Closer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Applied Door Closer</li><li>Concealed Door Closer</li><li>Floor Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Door Closer market is typically categorized into three main types: Surface Applied Door Closers, Concealed Door Closers, and Floor Springs. Surface Applied Door Closers are visible on the surface of the door and frame, providing a simple and effective closing solution. Concealed Door Closers are hidden within the door and frame for a more aesthetically pleasing appearance. Floor Springs are installed in the floor beneath the door, offering the advantage of being completely concealed and suitable for heavy doors. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
