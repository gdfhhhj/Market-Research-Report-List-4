<p><p>Laboratory cell sorting equipment market includes types such as Fluorescent Activated Cell Sorting (FACS), Magnetic-activated Cell Sorting (MACS), and MEMS - Microfluidics. FACS uses fluorescence detection to sort cells based on specific biomarkers. MACS utilizes magnetic beads to separate cells based on surface markers. MEMS-Microfluidics technology allows for precise manipulation of cells within microscale channels. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of efficiency, accuracy, and scalability for researchers and healthcare professionals working with cell populations.</p></p>
