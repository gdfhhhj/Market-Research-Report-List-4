<p><p>Digital Metering Pumps come in two main types: Electromagnetic Drive and Motor Drive. Electromagnetic Drive pumps use electromagnetic force to precisely control the flow rate, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. Motor Drive pumps, on the other hand, use a motor to regulate the flow, offering flexibility and ease of control. Both types of pumps are widely used in various industries such as water treatment, chemical processing, and agriculture for accurate and efficient dosing of liquids.</p></p>
