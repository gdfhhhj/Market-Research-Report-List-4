<p><p>Engineered Presswood pallets come in two main types: Nestable Pallets and Rackable Pallets. Nestable pallets are designed to nest inside each other when empty, saving space during storage and transportation. This makes them ideal for industries with limited storage space. Rackable pallets, on the other hand, are designed to be used in racking systems, allowing for easy storage and organization of goods. This makes them suitable for industries with high storage capacity needs and frequent handling of pallets.</p></p>
