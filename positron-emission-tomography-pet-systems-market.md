<p><p>Positron Emission Tomography (PET) Systems market includes three types of imaging techniques: Direct Imaging, Indirect Imaging, and Alternative Imaging. Direct Imaging involves the detection of the gamma rays emitted directly by the positron-emitting radiopharmaceuticals. Indirect Imaging uses scintillation crystals to detect the annihilation photons formed from the interaction of the positron and electron. Alternative Imaging techniques include time-of-flight imaging and partial volume correction methods, which improve the resolution and accuracy of PET imaging.</p></p>
