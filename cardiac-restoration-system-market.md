<p><p>Cardiac restoration systems are medical devices designed to repair and improve the function of the heart. Mitral Valve Restoration Systems specifically target the mitral valve, which controls blood flow between the left atrium and left ventricle. Left Ventricular Restoration Systems focus on restoring function to the left ventricle, the chamber responsible for pumping oxygenated blood to the rest of the body. Both types of devices aim to improve heart function and overall cardiovascular health in patients with heart conditions.</p></p>
