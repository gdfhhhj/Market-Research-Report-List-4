<p><p>Anesthetic Gas Scavenging System (AGSS) is a system used in hospitals to safely remove excess anesthetic gases from operating rooms. There are two types of AGSS systems: Single Blower and Double Blower. </p><p>Single Blower systems use one blower to pull and vent the gases outside the hospital, while Double Blower systems have two blowers for increased efficiency and backup in case of blower failure. Both systems are crucial in maintaining a safe and healthy environment for patients and healthcare workers by preventing exposure to excess anesthetic gases.</p></p>
