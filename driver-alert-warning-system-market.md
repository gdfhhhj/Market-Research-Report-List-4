<p><p>The Driver Alert Warning System is a safety feature in vehicles designed to monitor driver behavior and detect signs of drowsiness or inattention. This system uses various sensors and cameras to track the movement of the driver's eyes, facial expressions, and steering patterns to determine if the driver is showing signs of fatigue. When the system detects potential danger, it alerts the driver through visual, audible, or haptic warnings to regain focus on the road.</p><p>The Driver Alert Warning System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period.
