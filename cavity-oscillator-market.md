<p><p>Cavity Oscillator refers to the market for devices that generate electromagnetic waves using a resonant cavity. There are two main types in this market: Microwave Chamber and Radio Frequency Cavity oscillators. Microwave Chamber oscillators are designed for generating high-frequency electromagnetic waves, commonly used in radar and communication systems. Radio Frequency Cavity oscillators, on the other hand, utilize a resonant cavity to generate radio frequency signals, widely used in applications like cellular communication and broadcasting. Both types play important roles in various industries requiring precise and stable frequency generation.</p></p>
