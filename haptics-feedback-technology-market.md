<p><p>Haptics feedback technology market consists of various types including Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) actuators, Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs), drivers & controllers, software, and others. ERM actuators use an unbalanced mass to generate vibrations, while LRAs use a spring-mass system. Drivers & controllers provide the necessary power and control for haptic feedback systems, and software enables customization and integration of haptic effects. Other types may include advanced actuators or specialized components for specific applications within the haptics market.</p></p>
