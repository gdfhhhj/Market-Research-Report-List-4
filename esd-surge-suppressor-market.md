<p><p>ESD surge suppressors are devices used to protect electronic equipment from damaging electrostatic discharges. There are three main types of ESD surge suppressors: switch type, pressure limiting type, and other market types. Switch type suppressors use a switch mechanism to divert excess energy away from the equipment. Pressure limiting suppressors limit the amount of pressure that can build up during a surge. Other market types include a variety of different technologies and designs aimed at providing surge protection for specific applications or industries.</p></p>
