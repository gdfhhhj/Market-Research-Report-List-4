<p><p>Quadrant PIN photodiodes are classified into two main types based on the material used in their construction - InGaAs PIN photodiodes and Silicon PIN photodiodes. InGaAs PIN photodiodes are made from Indium Gallium Arsenide material, which offers high sensitivity in the near-infrared region. Silicon PIN photodiodes, on the other hand, are made from silicon material, providing good sensitivity in the visible and near-infrared regions. Both types of photodiodes are widely used in various applications such as telecommunications, medical devices, and industrial sensing.</p></p>
