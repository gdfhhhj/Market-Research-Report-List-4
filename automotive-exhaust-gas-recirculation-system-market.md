<p><p>Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation System market consists of two main types of EGR valves: Pneumatic EGR Valve and Electric EGR Valve. Pneumatic EGR valves are operated by air pressure and are commonly found in older vehicles. On the other hand, Electric EGR valves are electronically controlled and are more commonly used in modern vehicles for better precision and efficiency. Both types of EGR valves serve the purpose of reducing emissions and improving fuel efficiency in vehicles by recirculating exhaust gas back into the engine.</p></p>
