<p><p>Laboratory sodium ion meters are available in two main types - single input and dual input meters. Single input meters are designed to measure sodium ions in one sample at a time, making them ideal for laboratories with low sample throughput. On the other hand, dual input meters can measure sodium ions in two samples simultaneously, increasing efficiency for labs with higher sample volumes. Both types of meters provide accurate and reliable measurements of sodium ions in laboratory settings.</p></p>
