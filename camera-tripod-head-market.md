<p><strong>The Camera Tripod Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Removable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed camera tripod heads are permanently attached to the tripod legs and cannot be removed or replaced. These types of heads are typically more affordable and are suitable for general photography needs. Removable camera tripod heads, on the other hand, can be easily attached and detached from the tripod legs. This allows photographers to switch between different types of heads depending on their specific needs, such as ball heads, pan-tilt heads, or gimbal heads. Removable heads are more versatile but may be more expensive than fixed heads.</p></p>
