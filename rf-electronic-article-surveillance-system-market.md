<p><p>RF Electronic Article Surveillance System Market includes various types of products such as hard tags, soft tags, deactivators or detachers, detection systems, and permanent deactivation tags. Hard tags are durable and reusable, while soft tags are more discreet and easily attachable to the product. Deactivators or detachers are used to disable the tags at the point of sale. Detection systems alert staff to any activated tags leaving the store. Permanent deactivation tags are one-time use and cannot be reactivated after purchase.</p></p>
