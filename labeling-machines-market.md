<p><p>Labeling machines can be classified into two main types: automatic labeling machines and semi-automatic labeling machines. Automatic labeling machines are fully automated and can apply labels to products quickly and efficiently without much human intervention. They are ideal for high-volume production environments. On the other hand, semi-automatic labeling machines require some level of human involvement to apply labels to products. They are more cost-effective for small to medium-sized businesses with lower production volumes. Both types of labeling machines play a crucial role in streamlining the labeling process for businesses.</p></p>
