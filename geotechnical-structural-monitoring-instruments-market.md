<p><p>Geotechnical and Structural Monitoring Instruments are essential for assessing the stability and safety of infrastructure projects. Inclinometers measure the slope of the ground, while Extensometers monitor changes in soil or rock deformation. Piezometers gauge water pressure in the ground. Other instruments include settlement plates, crack gauges, and fiber optic sensors. These tools provide crucial data for engineers and construction professionals to make informed decisions and ensure the structural integrity of buildings, dams, tunnels, and other civil engineering projects.</p></p>
