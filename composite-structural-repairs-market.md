<p><p>Composite structural repairs market types refer to different methods used for repairing composite structures. Hand lay-up involves manually layering resin and reinforcement materials. Vacuum infusion uses a vacuum to impregnate resin into reinforcement materials. Autoclave repair involves curing composite materials under heat and pressure. Other methods may include filament winding, resin transfer molding, or prepreg lay-up. Each method offers unique benefits and is suited for different types of composite structural repairs based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
