<p><p>EOOE converters are available in two main market types: single mode and multimode. Single mode converters are designed for long-distance transmission, typically over 10 km, with a narrow core fiber that allows for higher bandwidth and less signal loss. Multimode converters, on the other hand, are suitable for shorter distances, typically under 500 meters, using a larger core fiber for lower-cost, high-speed applications. Both types offer different solutions for various transmission needs in networking and telecommunications.</p></p>
