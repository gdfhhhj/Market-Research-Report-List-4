<p><p>Actuator cables come in two main types: elbow and straight. Elbow actuator cables are designed with a 90-degree bend, making them suitable for installations where a sharp turn is needed. On the other hand, straight actuator cables are simply straight without any bends. Both types are used in a variety of applications where precise and efficient control of mechanical systems is required. The choice between elbow and straight actuator cables depends on the specific needs of the installation and the equipment being used.</p></p>
