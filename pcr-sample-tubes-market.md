<p><strong>The PCR Sample Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-bottom Tubes</li><li>Cylindrical Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCR sample tubes are available in two main types: V-bottom tubes and cylindrical tubes. V-bottom tubes have a conical shape at the bottom which allows for more efficient sample recovery and minimizes sample loss during centrifugation. Cylindrical tubes, on the other hand, have a straight and uniform shape which makes them compatible with automated liquid handling systems. Both types of tubes are widely used in molecular biology research for PCR and other applications due to their convenience, reliability, and compatibility with various laboratory equipment.</p></p>
