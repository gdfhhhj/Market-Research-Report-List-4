<p><p>Hydraulic Magnetic Circuit Breakers are divided into two main types based on the number of poles: Single-pole and Multi-pole. Single-pole circuit breakers are designed to interrupt the circuit in one single pole, while multi-pole circuit breakers can interrupt the circuit in multiple poles simultaneously. Single-pole circuit breakers are commonly used in applications where only one pole needs to be interrupted, while multi-pole circuit breakers are suitable for applications where multiple poles need to be protected simultaneously. Both types offer reliable overcurrent protection in various electrical systems.</p></p>
