<p><p>Overcurrent circuit protectors are devices designed to protect electrical circuits from excess current flow. There are three main types of overcurrent circuit protectors: fast melt protectors, slow melt protectors, and semiconductor protectors. Fast melt protectors respond quickly to high currents by melting a fuse wire. Slow melt protectors have a thicker fuse wire that melts more slowly, providing protection against short-duration overloads. Semiconductor protectors use semiconductor technology to quickly interrupt the current flow in case of an overload, making them ideal for sensitive electronic devices.</p></p>
