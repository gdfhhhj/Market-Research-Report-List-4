<p><p>Needlefree Closed System Transfer Devices (CSTD) include Closed Vial Access Devices, Closed Syringe Safety Devices, and Closed Bag/Line Access Devices. Closed Vial Access Devices are designed to maintain a closed system when accessing medication from vials. Closed Syringe Safety Devices prevent accidental needlesticks by ensuring the needle is safely contained after use. Closed Bag/Line Access Devices allow for the transfer of fluids without the risk of contamination. These different types of CSTDs offer healthcare professionals a variety of options to safely administer medications and handle hazardous drugs.</p></p>
