<p><p>Non-torque type electric rolling motor market consists of motors that are used for applications where constant speed is required, such as in fans and pumps. These motors do not provide additional torque to the system. On the other hand, torque type electric rolling motor market includes motors that are used for applications where varying torque is needed, such as in conveyors and cranes. These motors are capable of providing additional torque to meet the requirements of the system. Both types of motors play crucial roles in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
