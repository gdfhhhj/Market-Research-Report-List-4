<p><p>A slipper clutch, also known as a back-torque limiter or anti-hop clutch, is a specialized clutch system used in motorcycles and high-performance vehicles. It is designed to prevent excessive engine braking and rear wheel hop during aggressive downshifting or sudden deceleration. The slipper clutch works by allowing the clutch to partially disengage or &lsquo;slip&rsquo; when the rear wheel tries to drive the engine faster than it would in normal conditions, reducing the chances of lock-up and loss of control.</p><p>The Slipper Clutch Market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing demand for high-performance motorcycles and sports cars across the globe. Manufacturers in the market are focusing on research and development activities to enhance the performance and efficiency of slipper clutches, further fueling market growth.</p></p>
