<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber</li><li>Large-Tow Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber refers to a type of carbon fiber material that is made up of numerous small-tow fibers, typically ranging from 3K to 24K. This type of carbon fiber is widely used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods due to its high strength and lightweight properties. </p><p>On the other hand, Large-Tow Carbon Fiber is made up of fewer but larger tow fibers, typically ranging from 48K to 144K. This type of carbon fiber is preferred for applications where high stiffness and impact resistance are required, such as in wind energy and automotive industries.</p></p>
