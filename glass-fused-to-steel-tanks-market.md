<p><p>Glass Fused to Steel Tanks come in two main market types: Two-Layer Sheet and Single-Layer Sheet. Two-Layer Sheet tanks have an inner sheet of glass fused to steel and an outer sheet made of enamel. These tanks are known for their durability and resistance to corrosion. Single-Layer Sheet tanks, on the other hand, consist of a single sheet of glass fused to steel, offering a more cost-effective option while still providing the benefits of corrosion resistance and long-lasting performance.</p></p>
