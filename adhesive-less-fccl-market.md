<p><p>Adhesive-less FCCL (Flexible Copper Clad Laminate) can be categorized into single-sided and double-sided market types. Single-sided FCCL consists of a single layer of conductive copper foil bonded to a flexible substrate, making it suitable for simpler electronic applications. On the other hand, double-sided FCCL features a conductive copper layer on both sides of the substrate, offering increased circuit complexity and flexibility. Both market types cater to different electronic product requirements based on their design and functionality needs.</p></p>
