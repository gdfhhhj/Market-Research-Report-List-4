<p><p>Pharmaceutical freeze dryers are available in two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic freeze dryers are equipped with advanced technologies that control the entire freeze-drying process without the need for manual intervention. They offer greater efficiency, accuracy, and ease of operation. On the other hand, semi-automatic freeze dryers require some level of manual intervention and monitoring during the freeze-drying process. They are suitable for smaller-scale operations or for users who prefer more hands-on control over the process.</p></p>
