<p><p>X-Ray Inspection Systems for Packaged Product market includes two main types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). </p><p>Digital Radiography (DR) systems use digital detectors to create detailed images of the product, allowing for accurate inspection of packaged goods. This type of system is commonly used in the food industry to detect contaminants or defects in products.</p><p>Computed Tomography (CT) systems take cross-sectional images of the product, providing a 3D view that allows for thorough inspection of the entire package. This type of system is often used in industries where detailed imaging is crucial, such as pharmaceuticals or electronics.</p></p>
