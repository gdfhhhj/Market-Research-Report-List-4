<p><p>Front-end payment processors focus on the initial interaction between the customer and the payment system. They handle the front-end of the transaction, such as capturing card details and authentication. On the other hand, back-end payment processors deal with the processing and settlement of transactions behind the scenes. They ensure that the payment is routed correctly, funds are transferred between accounts, and that the transaction is ultimately approved or declined. Both front-end and back-end processors are critical components of the payment processing ecosystem.</p></p>
