<p><p>Drilling jumbos are used in mining and tunneling projects for rock drilling. The three main types of drilling jumbos are single-boom, two-boom, and multi-boom. Single-boom jumbos have one drilling arm, making them more compact and suitable for smaller tunnels. Two-boom jumbos have two arms for faster drilling in larger tunnels. Multi-boom jumbos have multiple arms, allowing for simultaneous drilling in different directions. Each type has its specific advantages and is used based on the size and complexity of the project.</p></p>
