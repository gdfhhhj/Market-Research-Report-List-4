<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared Curing Lamp market can be categorized into two main types based on the direction of the lamp's emission of infrared rays. Lengthwise Direction market refers to lamps that emit infrared rays along the length of the object being cured, allowing for uniform and thorough curing. Lateral Direction market, on the other hand, refers to lamps that emit rays perpendicular to the length of the object, providing targeted curing in specific areas. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the curing requirements of the application.</p></p>
