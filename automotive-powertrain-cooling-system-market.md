<p><p>The Automotive Powertrain Cooling System Market consists of different types such as ATOC (Air to Oil Cooling), ATF (Air to Transmission Fluid Cooling), ITOC (Impeller to Oil Cooling), and Others. ATOC utilizes airflow to cool oil in the system, while ATF involves cooling transmission fluid through air. ITOC uses an impeller to circulate oil for cooling purposes. Other types may include various methods of cooling different components within the powertrain system, providing options for manufacturers to choose the most suitable cooling solution based on the specific requirements of the vehicle.</p></p>
