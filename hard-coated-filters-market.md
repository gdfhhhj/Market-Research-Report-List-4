<p><strong>The Hard-Coated Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longpass Filter</li><li>Shortpass Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hard-Coated Filters are optical filters made with a durable coating to enhance their performance and longevity. There are two main types in the market: Longpass Filters and Shortpass Filters. Longpass Filters allow longer wavelengths to pass through while blocking shorter wavelengths. These filters are used in applications where blocking unwanted shorter wavelengths is crucial. On the other hand, Shortpass Filters allow shorter wavelengths to pass through while blocking longer wavelengths. These filters are used in applications where only specific shorter wavelengths are desired.</p></p>
