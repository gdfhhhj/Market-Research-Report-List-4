<p><ul><li>GPS Based Chart Plotter</li><li>GNSS Based Chart Plotter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPS Based Chart Plotter: These chart plotters use the Global Positioning System (GPS) to determine the vessel's location and plot it on a digital map. They offer accurate positioning information and are widely used in marine navigation.</p><p>GNSS Based Chart Plotter: These chart plotters use Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS), which include additional satellite systems such as GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou. They provide more precise positioning data and improved reliability compared to GPS-based systems. GNSS-based chart plotters are becoming increasingly popular in the marine industry for their enhanced performance.</p></p>
