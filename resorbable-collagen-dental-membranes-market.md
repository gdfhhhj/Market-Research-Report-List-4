<p><p>Resorbable collagen dental membranes are categorized based on the collagen origin type, such as porcine collagen or bovine collagen, and the collagen type, including Type I, Type II, and Type III. Porcine collagen membranes are derived from pig tissue, while bovine collagen membranes come from cow tissue. Each collagen type offers specific properties and benefits for use in dental procedures. Type I collagen is commonly used for its strength and biocompatibility, Type II for its elasticity, and Type III for its flexibility.</p></p>
