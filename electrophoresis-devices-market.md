<p><p>Electrophoresis devices market can be categorized into two main types: Gel Electrophoresis Systems and Capillary Electrophoresis Systems. Gel Electrophoresis Systems utilize a gel matrix to separate molecules based on size, while Capillary Electrophoresis Systems use a narrow tube filled with a buffer solution to separate molecules based on charge and size. Both types of systems are widely used in research laboratories and clinical settings for DNA analysis, protein separation, and other applications in the field of molecular biology.</p></p>
