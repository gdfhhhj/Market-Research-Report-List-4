<p><ul><li>C5</li><li>C9</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic petroleum resin market can be categorized into three main types - C5, C9, and others. C5 resins are produced from cracked petroleum naphtha and have good adhesion and tackifying properties, making them ideal for hot melt adhesives. C9 resins are derived from by-products of steam cracking of naphtha and have excellent heat resistance and compatibility with polymers, commonly used in paints and coatings. The "others" category encompasses a variety of specialized resins with unique properties suited for specific applications.</p></p>
