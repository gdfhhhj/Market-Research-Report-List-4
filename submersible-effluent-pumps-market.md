<p><p>Submersible effluent pumps are categorized into four main types based on their function and design. External elimination pumps are used to remove effluent directly from a tank or pit. Internal elimination pumps are designed to transport effluent from one area to another inside a building. Half internal elimination pumps combine features of both external and internal elimination pumps. Industrial elimination pumps are specifically designed for heavy-duty industrial applications where high volumes of effluent need to be transferred efficiently.</p></p>
