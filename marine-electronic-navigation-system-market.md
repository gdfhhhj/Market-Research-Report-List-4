<p><strong>The Marine Electronic Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vector Chart (ENC)</li><li>Raster Chart (RNC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine Electronic Navigation System market includes two main types of chart systems: Vector Chart (ENC) and Raster Chart (RNC). ENC uses vectors to display information such as depths, buoys, and landmarks, allowing for dynamic scaling and easy updating. RNC, on the other hand, displays scanned images of paper charts, providing a familiar visual reference for sailors. Both types of charts have their unique advantages and are utilized by different segments of the maritime industry for navigation and safety purposes.</p></p>
