Asphalt content testers are used to measure the percentage of asphalt binder in pavement materials. Soil density testers are utilized to determine the compactness and strength of soil layers beneath pavements. Continuous friction testers assess the skid resistance of pavement surfaces. Other types of testers cater to different aspects of pavement quality, such as thickness, roughness, and permeability.</p></p>
