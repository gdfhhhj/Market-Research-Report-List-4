<p><p>Atomic absorption spectrophotometers are used in analytical chemistry to determine the concentration of metals in samples. The two main types are Flame Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers and Electrothermal Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers. </p><p>Flame Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers use a flame to atomize and excite the sample, while Electrothermal Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers use an electrothermal atomizer to heat the sample. Both types provide accurate and sensitive measurements of metal concentrations in various samples, making them essential tools in laboratories for quality control and research purposes.</p></p>
