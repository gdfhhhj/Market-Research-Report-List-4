Type B sprockets have a hub on one side for mounting to a shaft. Type C sprockets have hubs on both sides for dual shaft mounting. Type D sprockets are double duty sprockets with two sets of teeth for driving two separate chains. Each type serves specific purposes in industrial machinery and equipment.</p></p>
