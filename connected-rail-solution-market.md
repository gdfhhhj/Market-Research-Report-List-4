<p><p>Connected Rail Solutions, such as Active Train Control (PTC), Communication/Computer-Based Train Control (CBTC), and Automatic/Integrated Train Control (ATC), are systems designed to enhance rail safety, efficiency, and communication. PTC utilizes GPS and trackside communications to prevent train collisions and derailments. CBTC uses real-time data communication to optimize train operations and improve passenger flow. ATC integrates various control systems to automate train movements, reducing delays and improving overall system reliability. These market types cater to different aspects of rail operation, offering advanced technological solutions for the industry.</p></p>
