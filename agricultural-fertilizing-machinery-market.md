<p><p>The agricultural fertilizing machinery market includes two main types of equipment: spreaders and sprayers. Spreaders are used to evenly distribute fertilizers, seeds, or other materials across fields. They can be either broadcast spreaders, which scatter the material over a wide area, or drop spreaders, which deposit it in a precise line. Sprayers, on the other hand, are designed to apply liquid fertilizers or pesticides to crops in a targeted manner. Both spreaders and sprayers play crucial roles in enhancing crop productivity and yields in agriculture.</p></p>
