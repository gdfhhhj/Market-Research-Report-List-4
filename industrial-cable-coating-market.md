<p><ul><li>IntumescentCableCoatings</li><li>Non-IntumescentCableCoatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial cable coating market is divided into two main types - Intumescent Cable Coatings and Non-Intumescent Cable Coatings. </p><p>Intumescent Cable Coatings are designed to expand and form a protective char layer when exposed to high temperatures, providing fire protection to the cables. On the other hand, Non-Intumescent Cable Coatings do not expand when exposed to fire, but provide a protective layer to prevent the spread of fire and maintain the integrity of the cables. Both types are essential in ensuring safety and reliability in industrial settings.</p></p>
