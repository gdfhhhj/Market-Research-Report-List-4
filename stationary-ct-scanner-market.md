<p><ul><li>High-slice CT</li><li>Mid-slice CT</li><li>Low-slice CT</li><li>Cone-beam CT (CBCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary CT scanners are categorized based on the number of slices they can capture in a single rotation. High-slice CT scanners offer higher resolution and clarity, making them ideal for detailed imaging. Mid-slice CT scanners provide a balance between image quality and speed. Low-slice CT scanners are more cost-effective and suitable for basic imaging needs. Cone-beam CT (CBCT) scanners use a cone-shaped X-ray beam for 3D imaging, making them ideal for dental and orthopedic applications. Each type of CT scanner caters to different imaging requirements and budgets.</p></p>
