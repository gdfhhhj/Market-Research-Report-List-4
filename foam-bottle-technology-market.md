<p><p>Foam bottle technology market includes various types such as Extrusion Moulding Technology, Blow Moulding Technology, and others. Extrusion Moulding Technology involves forcing molten plastic through a die to create a specific shape. Blow Moulding Technology, on the other hand, uses air pressure to expand a hollow plastic tube into a mould cavity. The "Others" market consists of alternative technologies used for producing foam bottles, such as injection moulding or compression moulding. Each type of technology offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
