<p><p>Reciprocating Gait Orthosis (RGO) is designed to assist individuals with walking difficulties. There are three main types of RGOs in the market: Horizontal Cable RGO, Rocker Bar RGO, and Isocentric RGO. Horizontal Cable RGO uses a system of cables and pulleys to assist with walking motion. Rocker Bar RGO utilizes a rocker bar mechanism to simulate a natural walking motion. Isocentric RGO incorporates a rotating joint at the hip, allowing for a more natural gait pattern. Each type of RGO offers unique benefits for individuals with different mobility needs.</p></p>
