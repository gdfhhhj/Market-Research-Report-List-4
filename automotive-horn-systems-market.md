<p><ul><li>Electronic Horn</li><li>Air Horn</li><li>Electromagnetic Horns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive horn systems consist of various types, including electronic horns, air horns, and electromagnetic horns. Electronic horns use a circuit to generate a sound signal, while air horns use compressed air to create a loud noise. Electromagnetic horns produce sound through an electromagnetic coil that vibrates a diaphragm. Each type of horn system has its own advantages and applications, catering to different preferences and needs in the automotive industry. Overall, these systems play a crucial role in ensuring safety on the roads by alerting other drivers and pedestrians.</p></p>
