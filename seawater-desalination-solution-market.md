<p><p>The seawater desalination solution market includes various types such as reverse osmosis, multi-effect distillation, and others. Reverse osmosis involves forcing seawater through a semi-permeable membrane to separate salt and impurities. Multi-effect distillation uses multiple evaporation and condensation stages to produce fresh water from seawater. Other desalination methods may include electrodialysis and solar desalination. Each type of desalination technology offers unique benefits and is used based on factors such as location, cost, and efficiency.</p></p>
