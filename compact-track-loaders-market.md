<p><p>Compact Track Loaders (CTLs) come in two main market types: Tired Vertical and Tracked Vertical. Tired Vertical CTLs are equipped with rubber tires similar to a traditional skid steer loader, providing good traction on various surfaces but may struggle on rough terrains. Tracked Vertical CTLs, on the other hand, have tracks instead of tires, offering superior stability and traction on challenging terrains such as mud, snow, and uneven surfaces. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of users depending on their specific requirements.</p></p>
