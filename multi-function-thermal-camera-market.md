<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Multi-function Thermal Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Thermal Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-function thermal cameras are classified based on the wavelength of the thermal radiation they detect. Short-wave length thermal cameras operate in the range of 1-3 μm, suitable for high-temperature industrial applications. Mid-wave length cameras detect thermal radiation in the 3-5 μm range, ideal for general surveillance and security purposes. Long-wave length cameras detect radiation in the 8-14 μm range, commonly used in firefighting, search and rescue operations, and medical imaging. Each type of thermal camera offers specific advantages depending on the intended application.</p></p>
