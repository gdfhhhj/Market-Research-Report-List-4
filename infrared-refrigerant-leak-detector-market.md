<p><p>Infrared refrigerant leak detectors are categorized into three main types based on the type of refrigerant they are designed to detect: CFC, HFC, and HCFC leak detectors. CFC leak detectors specifically target chlorofluorocarbon refrigerants, HFC leak detectors are tailored for hydrofluorocarbon refrigerants, and HCFC leak detectors are designed to detect hydrochlorofluorocarbon refrigerants. Each type of detector is optimized to effectively identify leaks of the specific refrigerant it is intended for, providing targeted and accurate detection for different types of refrigeration systems.</p></p>
