<p><p>Advanced Power Modules refer to high-performance semiconductor devices that integrate multiple power electronics components into a single module. Two common types in the market are Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) and Power Integrated Modules (PIMs). IPMs combine power devices with control circuits for improved efficiency and reliability in applications such as motor drives and inverters. PIMs are similar but focus on simpler power conversion functions. Both types offer compact, cost-effective solutions for power management in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
