<p><p>Portable grinders are tools used to remove material from surfaces or create a smooth finish. They come in three main types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic portable grinders are powered by compressed air, electric grinders use electricity, and hydraulic grinders are powered by hydraulic pressure. Each type has its own advantages and applications depending on the task at hand. Pneumatic grinders are typically lighter and more portable, electric grinders are more powerful, and hydraulic grinders are suitable for heavy-duty tasks.</p></p>
