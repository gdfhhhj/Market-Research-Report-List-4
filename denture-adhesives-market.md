<p><strong>The Denture Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Restoration Bonding</li><li>Indirect Restoration Bonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Denture adhesives market consists of two types: Direct Restoration Bonding and Indirect Restoration Bonding. Direct Restoration Bonding involves the application of adhesive directly to the surface of the denture to improve its fit and stability. On the other hand, Indirect Restoration Bonding involves using adhesives to attach a restoration, such as a crown or bridge, to the natural teeth or dental implants. Both types of adhesives play a crucial role in ensuring the longevity and effectiveness of dental restorations.</p></p>
