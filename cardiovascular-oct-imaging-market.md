<p><p>Cardiovascular OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) Imaging is a medical imaging technique that uses light waves to capture high-resolution, cross-sectional images of the blood vessels in the cardiovascular system. This technology allows for detailed visualization of the vessel walls and is used in the diagnosis and treatment of various cardiovascular conditions such as atherosclerosis, coronary artery disease, and stent placement.</p><p>The Cardiovascular OCT Imaging Market is experiencing rapid growth, with a projected CAGR of 15.34% during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, growing demand for minimally invasive procedures, and advancements in imaging technology are driving the market growth. Furthermore, the rise in healthcare expenditure, improving healthcare infrastructure, and increasing awareness about early disease detection are also contributing to the market expansion.</p><p>The latest trends in the Cardiovascular OCT Imaging Market include the development of next-generation OCT systems with improved image quality and faster scanning speeds, as well as the integration of artificial intelligence for automated image analysis. Additionally, there is a growing focus on expanding the application of OCT imaging beyond coronary arteries to other areas of the cardiovascular system, such as peripheral arteries and structural heart disease. These trends are expected to further propel the market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
