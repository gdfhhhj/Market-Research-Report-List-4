<p><p>Rapid absorbable sutures market includes two main types: Natural absorbable sutures and Synthetic absorbable sutures. Natural absorbable sutures are made from materials like catgut or collagen that break down in the body over time. Synthetic absorbable sutures are typically made from materials like polymers or glycolide, which also degrade in the body. Both types are designed to be absorbed by the body, eliminating the need for removal later on. These sutures are commonly used in surgeries and medical procedures for wound closure.</p></p>
