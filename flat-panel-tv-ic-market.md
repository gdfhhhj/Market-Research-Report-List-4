Digital ICs control the digital functions of the TV, such as image processing and signal transmission. Analog ICs manage the analog functions like tuning and audio processing. Mixed Signal ICs combine both digital and analog functions, providing a comprehensive solution for flat panel TV technology. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the smooth operation and performance of the TV.</p></p>
