<p><p>In X-Radiation Tube Market, two main types are Stationary Anode and Rotating Anode tubes. Stationary Anode tubes have a fixed target that dissipates heat more slowly, making them suitable for lower power applications. On the other hand, Rotating Anode tubes have a rotating target that can handle higher power levels and heat dissipation, making them ideal for higher-quality imaging and longer duty cycles. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
