<p><p>Automotive Cabin Filters come in three main types: Particle filters, Activated Carbon filters, and Electrostatic filters. Particle filters are designed to trap large particles like dust and pollen. Activated Carbon filters absorb odors and gases. Electrostatic filters use an electrostatic charge to attract and trap particles. Each type of filter offers unique benefits depending on the specific needs of the vehicle and its occupants, such as improved air quality, reduced odors, and protection against harmful pollutants.</p></p>
