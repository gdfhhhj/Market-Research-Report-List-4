<p><p>Straight rod electric screwdrivers are available in two main types, L Type and T Type. L Type screwdrivers have a straight rod with a handle perpendicular to the rod, resembling the letter "L". They are commonly used for tasks requiring precision and control. T Type screwdrivers have a handle at the top of the rod, forming a "T" shape. These are preferred for tasks requiring a firmer grip and added torque. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the electric screwdriver market.</p></p>
