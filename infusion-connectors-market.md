<p><p>Infusion connectors are essential components used in medical devices to deliver fluids or medications to patients. The Y infusion connector allows for multiple lines to be connected to a single access point, increasing efficiency in medical procedures. The T infusion connector allows for two lines to be connected at a right angle, enabling simultaneous delivery of different fluids. The straight infusion connector is a simple, straight-line connector used for single-line fluid delivery. Each type of infusion connector serves a specific purpose in medical settings.</p></p>
