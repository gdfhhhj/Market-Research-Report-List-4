<p><p>Fish traps are commonly categorized into three market types based on the material they are made of: Nylon Type, PE Type, and PP Type. Nylon type fish traps are known for their durability and flexibility, making them ideal for trapping a variety of fish species. PE type traps are more rigid and resistant to chemicals and abrasions, but may have a higher cost. PP type traps are lightweight and resistant to UV rays, making them suitable for long-term use in harsh environments. Each type offers unique advantages for different fishing needs.</p></p>
