<p><p>Building Information Modelling (BIM) is a process that involves creating and managing digital representations of physical and functional characteristics of a built environment. BIM allows architects, engineers, and construction professionals to collaborate and visualize a building's design and construction before it is built. This helps in improving efficiency, reducing errors, and saving time and costs during the construction process.</p><p>The Building Information Modelling Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period. The increasing adoption of BIM technology in the construction industry, advancements in software solutions, and the growing trend of sustainable building practices are driving the market growth. Additionally, government initiatives promoting the use of BIM in construction projects to improve productivity and reduce wastage are further fueling the market expansion.</p><p>Key trends in the Building Information Modelling Market include the integration of cloud-based BIM solutions, the emergence of virtual reality and augmented reality applications for BIM visualization, and the growing popularity of BIM for facility management and maintenance. These trends are expected to continue shaping the BIM market in the coming years, driving further growth and innovation in the industry.</p></p>
