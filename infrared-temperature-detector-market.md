<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Infrared Temperature Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near Infrared and Short-Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared temperature detectors are categorized into three main types based on the wavelength used for temperature measurement. Near Infrared and Short-Wave Infrared detectors operate in the range of 0.7 to 3 µm, making them suitable for applications requiring high resolution. Mid-Wave Infrared detectors work in the range of 3 to 8 µm, offering a balance between resolution and temperature measurement range. Long-Wave Infrared detectors operate in the range of 8 to 14 µm, providing accurate temperature readings for objects at longer distances.</p></p>
