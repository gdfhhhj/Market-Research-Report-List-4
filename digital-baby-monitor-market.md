<p><p>Interactive digital baby monitors allow parents to not only hear but also see their baby through a camera and screen, enabling two-way communication. This type of monitor typically includes features such as temperature sensors, lullabies, and remote pan/tilt/zoom functions. On the other hand, non-interactive digital baby monitors only provide audio monitoring without any visual capabilities. They are simpler in design and functionality, usually consisting of a transmitter in the baby's room and a receiver for the parents to listen in on their baby's sounds.</p></p>
