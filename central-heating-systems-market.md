<p><p>Central heating systems are heating systems that provide warmth to an entire interior space, such as a building or home, from one point to multiple rooms. These systems usually consist of a central boiler or furnace that heats water, steam, or air, which is then distributed through a network of pipes or ducts to various rooms. The key components of central heating systems include boilers, radiators, pumps, and thermostats.</p><p>The central heating systems market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for energy-efficient heating solutions in residential and commercial buildings. The rising awareness about environmental sustainability and the need to reduce energy consumption are driving the adoption of central heating systems.
