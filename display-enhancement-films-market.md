<p><p>Display Enhancement Films market can be categorized into two main types: Passive Enhancement and Active Enhancement. Passive Enhancement films are non-electronic and work by reflecting or absorbing light to improve screen clarity and reduce glare. On the other hand, Active Enhancement films use electronic components to enhance image quality by dynamically adjusting brightness, contrast, and color. While Passive Enhancement films are more cost-effective and easier to install, Active Enhancement films provide more advanced features and customization options for users.</p></p>
