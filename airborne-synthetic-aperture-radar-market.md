<p><p>Airborne Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) systems are categorized into two market types based on their operating modes: Single Mode and Multi-Mode. Single Mode SAR systems operate in either spotlight or stripmap mode, providing high-resolution images for detailed target analysis. Multi-Mode SAR systems, on the other hand, offer the flexibility to switch between different operating modes, such as spotlight, stripmap, wide-area, and inverse SAR, allowing for a wide range of applications and operational scenarios. Both types cater to different user requirements and preferences in the airborne SAR market.</p></p>
