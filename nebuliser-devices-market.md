<p><p>Nebuliser devices are commonly categorized into three main types: pneumatic nebulisers, ultrasonic nebulisers, and mesh nebulisers. Pneumatic nebulisers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation. Ultrasonic nebulisers utilize high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist of medication. Mesh nebulisers use a vibrating mesh to generate droplets for inhalation. Each type of nebuliser device has its own advantages and can be used for different medical conditions, providing efficient and effective delivery of medication to patients.</p></p>
