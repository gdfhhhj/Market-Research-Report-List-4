<p><p>Pyrolytic Graphite (PG) market is divided into two main types - Sheet and Powder. PG sheets are thin layers of graphite material with high thermal conductivity and electrical resistance, used in industries such as electronics and aerospace. On the other hand, PG powder consists of fine particles of pyrolytic graphite, utilized in applications like lubricants and coatings. Both types cater to different market needs and provide diverse solutions for various industries requiring high-performance graphite materials.</p></p>
