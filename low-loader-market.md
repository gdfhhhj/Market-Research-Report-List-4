<p><p>Low loader markets can be divided into two main types: tire type and crawler type. </p><p>Tire type low loaders are equipped with wheels that allow for easy transport on roads and highways. They are commonly used for short to medium distance transportation of heavy equipment and machinery.</p><p>Crawler type low loaders, on the other hand, are equipped with tracks similar to those on tanks. This allows them to navigate rough terrain and access construction sites that may be difficult to reach with tire type low loaders.</p><p>Both types of low loaders serve the purpose of transporting heavy equipment efficiently, but cater to different types of terrain and transportation needs.</p></p>
